Healthy Food at 7-Eleven Japan

If you want to eat healthy while traveling in Japan, 7-Eleven has many good options.

Best Healthy Combo

🥗 Pack Salad + 🐔 Salad Chicken + 🍙 Salmon Onigiri

💡 Tip: Salad Chicken is usually near the drinks section.

Why this works: Low fat, balanced nutrition, and easy to find.

💴 Around 700-900 yen total

You can ask staff to heat your food. No Japanese needed.

💳 Most stores accept credit cards and IC cards.
