Cheap Meals at Japanese Convenience Stores

If you are traveling in Japan and want to save money, convenience stores are your best friend.

Best Budget Combo (~500-600 yen)

🍙 2 Rice Balls (Onigiri) + 🍜 Cup Soup

Why this works: Cheap, filling, and available 24/7.

You can find this at 7-Eleven, FamilyMart, and Lawson.
